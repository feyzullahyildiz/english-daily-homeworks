## 📐 Paralel Konular (Cümle Genişletme)

Her paralel konu 2-3 gün boyunca ana konuyla birlikte işlenir.

1. That-clause — *I know that you like cats.*
2. Gerund as Subject — *Loving cats is hard.*
3. Infinitive as Subject — *To own a cat is difficult.*
4. Embedded Question — *I know when you started loving cats.*
5. Object Complement — *I found it hard to love cats.*
6. Time Clause — *When I was young, I loved cats.*
7. Relative Clause — *The cat that I love is small.*
8. Reported Speech (basit) — *She said she loved cats.*
9. Causative — *I made him love cats.*
10. Concession — *Although it's hard, I love cats.*
